EXHORTATION

During exhortation, the priest addresses the congregation on biblical matters that are important to parishioners.

COUNSELING

Counseling at St. Matthew Catholic Church, Maryland, Lagos offers both biblical guidance and professional counseling.

Morning Prayer

Prayer is important because it makes us more like Jesus and because it reveals to us the heart and mind of God.

Mass

Mass is the central act of worship in the life of a Catholic. Going to Mass is about spending time with God, but also receiving his graces.

Sunday Masses

Our church provides regular Sunday Mass. Feel free to find out more about the exact dates and times on our site.

Weddings

You can easily make one of life's most memorable moments fully unforgettable by hosting a wedding at St. Agnes Catholic Church, Maryland, Lagos.

Funerals

A funeral mass is often held at the church of the deceased with a public viewing before the church mass.

Baptisms

The sacrament of Baptism not only gives us sanctifying grace; it also makes us adopted children of God and heirs of heaven.
