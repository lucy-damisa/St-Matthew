WELCOME TO ST MATTHEW AMUKOKO

A visit to the Amukoko surroundings testifies to the huge population of persons majorly made up of the Igbos, and the Yorubas. Although, being in a Yoruba territory, the huge population of other tribes especially the igbos, can not be overlooked. St Matthew Catholic Church Amukoko, a parish blessed with parishioners who cut across the various tribes in the densely populated area of Lagos state, is blessed with good and talented people. I assure you that a visit to this parish will keep a lasting unforgettable memory of our hospitality in your mind. St Matthew is a parish that nutures the love of God and the love of neighbours in one's heart. Without overlooking the human frailties and struggles prevalent in any human community, the love of God, no doubt, reigns supreme in our parish community. Welcome to our website. I hope you will find the information here very interesting and I hope that you will find time to visit us and worship with us physically. God bless you.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the significance of the Catholic Mass?
The Catholic Mass is a sacred liturgy where Catholics believe they participate in the sacrificial offering of Jesus Christ and receive the Eucharist, which they hold to be the true body and blood of Christ.
What is the purpose of confession in the Catholic Church?
Confession, or the Sacrament of Reconciliation, is a practice where Catholics confess sins to a priest and receive absolution, restoring their relationship with God and the Church.
Why do Catholics pray to saints?
Catholics believe saints can intercede on their behalf, praying to them for guidance and support, while ultimately directing worship to God.
What is the significance of the Rosary?
The Rosary is a form of prayer using a set of beads that helps Catholics meditate on the life of Jesus and the role of Mary, focusing on events called mysteries.
